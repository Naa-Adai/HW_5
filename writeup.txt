Implemented Features

1. Board is droppable
2. Board recognized double word and double letter bonuses
3. Words dropped into the board are recognized and displayed in program
4. Score is calculated accurately when next is clicked
5. Player can keep playing until the choose to restart game or pieces run out
6. The tiles can be returned to rack. 
7. Board is cleared when next or clear is clicked.
8. If tile is placed at an invalid position, it returns to its previous position before it was moved.
